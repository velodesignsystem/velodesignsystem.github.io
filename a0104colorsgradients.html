The Velo Spectrum
Our app has 2 different backgrounds; On-Dark, and On-White, with a range of intermediary screens that mix both white and dark together, as shown above on the Velo spectrum line.

The dark gradient is a strong brand indicator for our audience, allowing them to quickly identify they are in the Velo app. Typically dark gradient screens have shorter conversational content and short forms, allowing quick navigation through low value interactions. A majority of our app is presented with an On-Dark background.

The white background screens are typically used on information heavy screens. White backgrounds with dark text have significantly higher legibilty rates (26% increased legibility). It is important that users have a clear understanding of high value interactions and information. The use of white background is limited to a few sections of our app.
