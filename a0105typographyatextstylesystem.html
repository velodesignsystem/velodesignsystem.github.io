The Velo text style system
Our app uses Nunito Sans as our primary font for english text. In an effort to reduce variations of the font used in the app, we created a text style system that is outlined below.
This text style system does not include alignment, line height or opacity as part of its style, so developers will have to refere to screens in zeplin to gather that information.

For Chinese text, our app will use the deviceâ€™s default Chinese sans serif font.
As a financial banking app, it is important our dollar sign is distinctive and easy to recognize. It will be used in many places throughout the app, and is perhaps the most important symbol for all our customers.
To further reduce complexity and workload, we will very soon be creating our own version of Nunito Sans called Velito Sans, where the dollar sign has a strike through.
