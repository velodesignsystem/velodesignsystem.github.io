Background component
We have two kinds of backgrounds, On-Dark and On-White.

For anyone using our Sketch Velo Design Library, these global components can be accessed by following the sketch paths below:
Sketch path:
Sketch> Insert>Velo Design Library > On-Dark > 01 Global > 01 Bkgnd
Sketch>Insert Velo Design Library > On-White > 01 Global > 01 iOS > 01 Bkgnd
Gradient consistency
It is important to maintain gradient consistency from screen to screen, as shown above the three screens are of different sizes, but the gradient color remains consistent between screens. This consistency ensures a smooth transistion between screens for users.
The image above shows the gradient component in all its various sizes, and the hex values at different heights. The gradient is diagonal, and after the end of the iphone 8 screen bottom, there is only a single solid blue color that continues down for long screens.
