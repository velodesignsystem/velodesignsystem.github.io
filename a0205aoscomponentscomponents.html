OS components -iOS
The symbols above show the range of iOS system components we have for our design library. The components include keyboards, notifications, notches, menus and status bars.

Our app will always utilize the dark keyboards, not the light keyboards, from the iOS system libary.

These components are only representations to communicate to developers the correct implementation. There should be no redesign or recreation of these components specifically for Velo.
