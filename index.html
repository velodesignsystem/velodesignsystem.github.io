The Velo Framework Design System is the vision from Velo by East West bank for the future of digital banking.
This framework style guide was created to provide clarity and invite a wide range of stakeholders to become valuable contributors to the creative process.
This design system was created from the collaborative efforts of various creative studios that include:
Definery studio, Meaningful Works studio, 17seconds studio and the
in-house design team at
Velo by East West Bank.
